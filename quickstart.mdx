This guide takes you from nothing to a Kiosk taking live orders. You'll set everything up from **Remy** on your phone, then register your iPad as a Kiosk. Most restaurants finish in under 20 minutes.

<Note>
  Before you start, have these on hand — you'll need all three to finish setup:

  - Your **smartphone** (to run Remy)
  - Your **iPad** (to run the Lumina Kiosk app)
  - Your **Square reader** (to take payments)
</Note>

## 1. Download Remy

Remy is Lumina's operator app where you set up and manage your kiosk.

- **[Download Remy](https://onelink.to/remy)**

## 2. Create your Lumina account

Open Remy and sign up to create your Lumina account and business.

## 3. Connect your Square account

Lumina runs on top of Square — your menu items, prices, and orders all live there.

1. From the home screen, tap the circle icon in the upper left corner to open the app menu. Tap **Square Integration** and then **Connect**.
2. Sign into your Square account and grant Lumina access to your Square account.
3. Remy pulls in your catalog automatically — you'll see **"Syncing with your Square library..."** with a progress indicator. This is a one-time sync on connection. It may take up to 10 minutes depending on the size of your catalog.

## 4. Create a menu

Once your menu has synchronized, from the **Menus** tab, create a new menu by tapping the "+" button. Remy gives you two ways to build it:

- **Create from Square Categories** — mirror categories you've already set up in Square. Fastest if your Square catalog is organized the way you want the Kiosk to look.
- **Add Menu Groups** — build groups by hand and add items individually. This allows you to create a custom menu layout for your kiosk.

Pick whichever matches your setup. You can adjust groups and items afterward.

## 5. Create a kiosk profile

A **profile** is the bundle a Kiosk runs — it ties together the menu, the welcome screen, and the security PIN, and it's what you assign to a device.

1. Create one with **Create a New Profile**.
2. Give it a name (e.g. "Front counter", "Patio").
3. You'll choose this profile when you register a Kiosk in the next steps.

## 6. Download the Lumina Kiosk app

On the iPad that will run as a Kiosk, install the **Lumina Kiosk** app from the App Store.

## 7. Register the Kiosk

Pair the iPad with your Remy account.

1. Open the Kiosk app on the iPad — it shows a QR code and a 6-character pairing code.
2. In Remy, go to the **Devices** tab and tap **Register a Kiosk**.
3. Pair using either method:
   - **Scan QR Code** with your phone, or
   - enter the **6-character pairing code** shown on the iPad.
4. **Tap to select location**, then **Tap to select profile** — choose the profile you created above.

## 8. Connect a Square reader

Finish by pairing a Square reader so the Kiosk can take payments.

1. On the iPad, tap **Add a Reader**.
2. Follow the on-screen prompts to pair your Square reader over Bluetooth.
3. Wait for the **Reader Connected** confirmation.

That's it — tap to start an order and run a test transaction.
