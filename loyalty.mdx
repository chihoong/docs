## How it works

- Your loyalty program — points, rewards, and earn rules — is set up in your Square account.
- Once you enable it on the Kiosk, the program is available to guests automatically — there's nothing to rebuild in Lumina.
- Enrollment runs through Square Loyalty (typically by phone number), so a guest's points follow them across your Square-powered channels.

## Enable Square Loyalty on the Kiosk

If you use Square Loyalty, turn it on for the Kiosk in Remy:

1. In Remy, tap the **circle icon in the upper left corner** to open the app menu.
2. Tap **Square Integration**.
3. Toggle **Loyalty Program** on.
4. Restart the Kiosk. Once it reloads, guests will see an **Earn Rewards** option next to the **View Order** button.

## Promote loyalty on the Kiosk menu

Beyond the built-in Earn Rewards button, you can spotlight loyalty on the menu itself using collections.

1. In Remy's **Menus** tab, open **Collections** and add a **Loyalty Program** and/or **Rewards** highlight (see [Menu management → Collections](/menu-management#collections)).
2. Set its cover image. As Remy notes, *"Tapping cover image will promote user signup"* — guests who tap it are taken into the rewards signup flow.
