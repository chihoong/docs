Most Kiosk issues come down to connectivity or the card reader. Work through the steps below, and if you're still stuck, reach the support team.

## Internet connectivity

A Kiosk that can't reach the internet shows **Offline** in Remy's **Devices** list. Work through these steps:

1. **Check Internet Connection** — make sure the iPad's Wi-Fi is active and stable.
2. **Restart Wi-Fi Router** — unplug the router, wait about 10 seconds, then plug it back in.
3. **Restart App** — reloads the Kiosk app without rebooting the iPad.
4. **Restart Device** — restarts the hardware; reopen the Kiosk app afterward.

## Reader connection

The Square reader pairs with the iPad over Bluetooth through Square's own flow. If payments aren't working or the reader won't connect:

1. From the troubleshoot options, choose **Square Payment or Reader** — *"Try reauthorizing or repairing from admin dashboard."*
2. Make sure Bluetooth is on and the reader is charged and in range.
3. Re-pair the reader: on the iPad tap **Add a Reader** and follow the prompts until you see **Reader Connected**.

If the reader still won't pair, restart the iPad (above) and try once more — Bluetooth pairing usually recovers after a reboot.
