You're moving this iPad from the classic **KIOSK** app to the new **Lumina Kiosk** app. Your Square connection, locations, and the menu you've already built in Remy carry over — you're just registering this device on the new app.

<Note>
  Before you start:

  - Have **Remy** installed on your phone.
  - Be in front of the kiosk — you'll go back and forth between the iPad and Remy.
  - Have a **kiosk profile** ready. If you don't have one yet, follow [Create a kiosk profile](/quickstart#5-create-a-kiosk-profile) first.
</Note>

## 1. Open Lumina Kiosk on the iPad

Look for the new **Lumina Kiosk** icon (shown above) on the iPad's home screen.

If the classic **KIOSK** app is already running in kiosk mode, quit it first — swipe up to open the app switcher and swipe the KIOSK app away. Then tap **Lumina Kiosk** to open it.

On first launch you'll see the pairing screen with a QR code and a 6-character pairing code.

## 2. Register the kiosk from Remy

1. In Remy, open the **Devices** tab.
2. Scroll to the bottom of the device list and tap **Register Django**.
3. **Scan QR Code** with your phone — or enter the **6-character pairing code** shown on the iPad.

## 3. Set the location and assign a profile

Still in Remy, finish pairing:

1. **Tap to select location** and choose this kiosk's location.
2. **Tap to select profile** and pick the profile you prepared.

## 4. Connect your Square reader

On the iPad, tap **Add a Reader** and follow the prompts to pair your Square reader over Bluetooth. Wait for the **Reader Connected** confirmation.

## 5. Start the kiosk

On the iPad, tap **Start Kiosk Mode**. The kiosk goes live on the new app — run a quick test order to confirm payments work.
