---
title: "How to update your kiosk software "
---

<Tip>
  If you're offsite or need to update multiple locations/kiosks, we recommend using Remy, our remote management app.&#x20;
</Tip>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Remotely via Remy">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Tap 'Update All Kiosks' in Remy > Kiosk tab " stepNumber="1">
        Your kiosk screen will turn off and then return to the Kiosk menu.  &#x20;

        This may take a few minutes to complete.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Enable 'Kiosk Mode'" stepNumber="2" />
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="In-store on the kiosk">
    1. Disable 'Kiosk Mode' in the Kiosk Dashboard (tap Back from Kiosk Settings). 

    2. Exit the Kiosk app and go to the 'Business' app.

    3. Update the Kiosk app and wait a few minutes for the download to finish.

           <img width="440" src="https://userimg-assets.customeriomail.com/images/client-env-125952/1734993740145_Screenshot%202024-12-23%20at%202.41.14%E2%80%AFPM_01JFTTZ0H2FHP356P64J6BXER2.jpeg" />

    4. Restart your kiosk with Remy.

    5. Enable 'Kiosk Mode' and Start your Kiosk.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
